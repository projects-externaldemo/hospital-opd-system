Diseases We Address

Overview of common conditions we help evaluate and manage—always paired with individualized medical advice.

Type 2 Diabetes

Chronic high blood sugar managed with nutrition, activity, medications, and routine A1c monitoring.

Hypertension

Elevated blood pressure that raises stroke and heart risk; treated with lifestyle steps and guided therapy.

Asthma

Reactive airways causing cough and wheeze; controlled with trigger reduction and personalized inhaler plans.

Heart Disease

Coronary and vascular conditions requiring risk-factor control, medications, and coordinated cardiology follow-up.

Arthritis

Joint pain and stiffness from wear-and-tear or inflammatory disease; care focuses on mobility and pain control.

Migraine

Recurring headaches with sensitivity to light or sound; prevention and rescue plans tailored to your pattern.

Thyroid Disorders

Hypo- or hyperthyroid states affecting energy and metabolism; diagnosed with labs and adjusted over time.

COPD

Chronic lung limitation from smoking or irritants; emphasis on inhaler technique, vaccines, and flare prevention.
